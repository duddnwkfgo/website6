March 22nd, 2018



I have slept in today. I got up at 9sh but then I decided to sleep again after taking a shower. So in the end, I got to the library at 11am-ish. Am I trying my best? That’s something that I want to ask myself at the end of the day and be able to say “HELL YEAH” with pride. But I know I'll never say "This is the best". I believe that I can always do better. You can always do better. In a way, I think, you are putting yourself a limit when you say “this is the best”

Anyways, yeah, uh, I am working on making my own website which is on my things-i-want-to-get-done-before-I-die list. I wanted to have my own website. And I didn’t want to use Wordpress or the service of those nature because I wanted to have originality. Well, that’s what I thought at first, thinking creating a website is all easy when I literally had no experience/education/knowledge about creating one. By watching Youtube videos and other online tutorials, I was able to create the website you are seeing now. Have I taken the easy-way by using Wordpress? No. Was I able to create one-of-a-kind website that I wanted? No. The front page is simply a copy-and-paste of source code from a HTML textbook that I was using an the navigation bar is the most generic navbar that you can see anywhere and the sliding image banner is also another work of copy-and-paste. Yeah, coding is a lot harder that I thought. Well, at least, there are some things I was able to learn from trying to create my own website. Hopefully, I will improve my skills and learn even more by continuing to make adjustments/improve my website.

So yeah, anyways, I wanted to have a platform where I could express myself and communicate with the world. I know that there existing platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Youtube and other social media but I thought I wanted to have my own. I mean, it just sounds better to say “Yo! Check out my website” instead of saying “Subscribe and follow me on (insert social network platform)”, right? Also it’s because I didn’t feel like spamming people on Facebook. **Yeah, I know. I am a motherf**ing hipster. I am SO HIP! WHEEEEE~

Anyways, there it is. It’s my first diary post on my website. It feels great. Yes. This is the feeling that I’ve been longing for –the sense of achievement. I hope to continue feeling this way for the rest of my life.
